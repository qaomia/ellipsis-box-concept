Welcome

Hi there. I’m Gabrielle and I had a little idea combining several passions of mine: imaginary universes, handcrafted fandom items and finding the perfect gifts for our loved ones.

This page will walk you through my idea. If it finds any echo within you, please contact me to chat!

What is it?

Have you ever felt powerless when reading the last page of your favorite book or watching the very last episode of your dear TV show?

The ellipsis box is a selection of carefully handcrafted items, all evolving around a common theme.

A universe that you don’t want to quit.

The ellipsis box is the dots that will let your favorite characters be integrated into the daily objects of your life.

And behind each item, there is a human that shares the same passion than you for this imaginary universe.

All ellipsis boxes will feature a little description of the crafters, the smiles and the little hands that created the very items you hold into your hands.
